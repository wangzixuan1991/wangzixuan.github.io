Candidate Tracker

A tool that allows employees to track interview candidates.

The Challenge

How to help interviewers keep track of their candidates?

This is a design challenge to create a web application design for their employees to track the interview candidates. The tool can be used for HR managers, interviewers and other employees curious about overall process. Suggested use cases are - HR manger inputs new candidates into the system, interviewer(s) review resumes and mark feedback, HR manager posts interview information, interviewers conduct interview and provide feedback, interviewers make hiring decision.

Prioritize

Focusing on One User Group - Interviewer

Due to the time limitation, I was asked to choose a most important user group to design for.

HR Manager

  • Second Priority
  • Oversee whole hiring process
  • Already tools existing

Interviewer

  • First Priority
  • Main decision maker
  • Busy and have other work to do

Other Employees

  • Third Priority
  • Not affecting hiring process

Research

What are the painpoints?

After choosing "interviewer" as the first-priority user group, I interviewed two interviewers to get first hand knowledge of their current experience and painpoints.
